Welcome to Visual Scrolling Aid's documentation!
================================================
Project was conceptuallised to help people scroll through documents with ease through the use of an eye tracking. This requires much less effort than scrolling through documents manually.

Requirements:
Our project depends on:

* OpenCV
* Tkinter
* pypdfocr
* ttk
* glob
* PIL
